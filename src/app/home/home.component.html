Software Developer

Before choosing development as a career path, I was a pick up and delivery truck driver for a manufacturing company, then moved into a shipping supervisor position in that company. I then moved into the steel industry for a couple years then Covid-19 hit. It was at that point that I decided to turn something I enjoyed and loved doing into a career path. I found and enrolled into Grand Circus front-end coding boot camp, where I solidified and cemented some self taught skills with HTML,CSS, and Javascript. I also learned some new skills like Angular, Typescript and SQL. I see the world moving father into tech, and I want to be a part making that happen by doing what I love, and enjoy, rather than just watching it happen.
